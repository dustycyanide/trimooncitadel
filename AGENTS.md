The goal for this project is to turn `landing-page.png` into `index.html`, an EXACT one to one match.

Keep re-iterating until we have reached that goal. Use the image generation model available through the codex app to create the image icons.

Important visual bug to avoid: do not add full-card gold, amber, or gradient pseudo-element overlays to the service cards or image feature cards when trying to improve ornate borders. This has repeatedly made cards look fully gold/tinted instead of dark blue with thin gold filigree. Ornamental corner work should be limited to transparent line-only borders, inset grooves, or tiny corner accents that cannot wash over the card face or artwork. After any border/corner change, render-check the Arcane Architecture card and both large feature image cards specifically.
